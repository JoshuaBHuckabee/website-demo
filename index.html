About Me

Hi, I'm a Web Developer at the University of North Texas with several years of hands-on experience building practical, often behind-the-scenes web solutions that keep our campus running smoothly. Whether it's cutting down on paper usage, streamlining clunky processes, or just making someone’s job a little easier, I love collaborating with departments to solve problems—sometimes with a bit of spaghetti code and a lot of heart.

Learning by doing has always been my approach. Maybe that’s part of what drew me to work at a university. It’s allowed me to experiment, adapt, and grow through a variety of real-world tech projects. Outside of work, I’m a husband, dad to a wild and wonderful 2-year-old, part-time rock climber, and full-time builder of raised beds and trellises for my wife’s thriving garden.
