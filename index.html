About Me

Hi, I'm a Web Developer at the University of North Texas with 8 years of hands-on experience building practical, often behind-the-scenes web solutions that keep our campus running smoothly. Whether it's cutting down on paper usage, streamlining clunky processes, or just making someone’s job a little easier, I love collaborating with departments to solve problems—sometimes with a bit of spaghetti code and a lot of heart.

I believe in learning by doing (true to the university spirit), and I’ve been lucky to explore a range of technologies along the way. Outside of work, I’m a husband, dad to a wild and wonderful 2-year-old, part-time rock climber, and full-time builder of raised beds and trellises for my wife’s thriving garden.
